Lux Mundis Cup 2024

The Lux Mundi Cup 2024 is a prestigious 5-day event organized by Tangub City Global College (TCGC) held from November 18-22, 2024, with the theme "Majestic Fusion." It features a diverse range of competitions, including academics (e.g., debate, writing), cultural special events (e.g., cheerdance, hip-hop, eSports, art), and sports (e.g., basketball, volleyball, badminton). It is celebrated with high energy and aims to promote camaraderie, excellence, and institutional pride.

The event was incredibly fun with engaging competitions. I participated in two events and had a blast. Throughout the event, I gained new experiences and made a lot of memories that I will cherish for a long time. The event not only allowed me to showcase my skills and have fun but also gave me the chance to connect with others, creating lasting friendships and moments to look back on.
